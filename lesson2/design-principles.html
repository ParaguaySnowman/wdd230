Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Walmart

walmart.com

The elements that Walmart, no doubt, considers most important are larger or called out with contrasting colors. Note the blue "add" call to action button. Also note the prices in bold text.

Hick's Law

Little Caesars

littlecaesars.com

Little Caesar's has used Hick's law to optimize the UX speed of the ordering process by keeping the number of decisions to be made on any one screen to a minimum.

Fitt's Law

Amazon Prime Music

music.amazon.com

Amazon Prime music player makes use of Fitt's Law to make music playing easy. It has combined the "play" button with the album thumbnail images, and on the music playing page has made the playing controls large and put in them in an easily-accessible location.
