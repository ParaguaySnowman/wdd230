IHCW.net

Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this document is to describe the IHCW.net website, its purpose and intended audience, utilization scenarios, and other aspects such as its organization and structure, elements of style, etc. It is to be used as a planning and elaboration tool for the personal site to be developed this semester as part of the WDD 230 course

Site Name

The site will be called IHCW.net. IHCW stands for "Idaho High Country Weather." As the name implies, the website is intended provide weather information for communities of the South-Eastern Idaho High Country, including Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Purpose Statement

The main purpose of the TrueWeather.net website will be to help community residents make short term plans based on current weather conditions and sound weather forecasts. The site will provide micro-weather conditions for three communities: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. There will also be a dedicated Storm Center.
